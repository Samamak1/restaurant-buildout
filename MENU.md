# Craft Cocktail Program

Six signature cocktails, all at $15, built around a tequila/mezcal backbone with house
syrups, fresh juice, and theatrical garnishes (flame, shimmer, dehydrated fruit). Menu
copy and full build specs below - as written for the bar team.

## Menu (guest-facing)

| Cocktail | Menu description |
|---|---|
| Sugar Skull | Blanco Tequila, Agavero Orange Liqueur, Lime Juice, Prickly Pear Puree, Plum Bitters |
| Oaxaca Flamed Old Fashioned | Reposado Tequila, Mezcal, Agave Syrup, Orange Bitters |
| Paloma | Mezcal, Lime Juice, Grapefruit Soda |
| Red Hibiscus Delight | Blanco Tequila, Agavero Orange Liqueur, Watermelon, Hibiscus, Lime, Rhubarb Bitters, Red Shimmer |

| House Margarita | Tequila, Lime Juice, Agave, Lemon Bitters |

## Build specs (bar-facing)

### Sugar Skull
- 1 1/2 oz blanco tequila
- 1 oz Agavero orange liqueur
- 1 oz freshly squeezed lime juice
- 1 oz prickly pear syrup
- 2 dashes plum bitters
- barspoon purple shimmer powder

Build all ingredients in a shaker tin. Pour into a cold martini glass. Garnish with a
card and clothespin.

### Oaxaca Flamed Old Fashioned
- 1 1/2 oz reposado tequila
- 1/2 oz mezcal
- 1/2 oz agave
- 2 dashes orange bitters

Build straight into a rocks glass over ice. Garnish with a flamed orange peel.

### Paloma
- 1 1/2 oz mezcal
- 1/2 oz lime juice
- top with grapefruit juice

Brush a strip of agave down the side of a Collins glass and dip in the salt-Tajin mix to
coat.

### Sweet Heat Mezcalita
- 1 1/2 oz mezcal
- 1 oz sweet heat syrup
- 1 1/2 oz orange juice
- 1/2 oz freshly squeezed lime juice
- 1/2 oz Agavero orange liqueur

Shake in a tin, strain into a Collins glass. Garnish with pineapple leaves and a
dehydrated orange slice.

### Red Hibiscus Delight
- 1 1/2 oz blanco tequila
- 1/2 oz Agavero orange liqueur
- 1 oz watermelon juice
- 1/2 oz hibiscus syrup
- 1/2 oz lime juice
- 2 dashes rhubarb bitters
- barspoon red shimmer powder

Short shake in a tin, pour over ice into a rocks glass. Garnish with a torched rosemary
sprig.

### House Margarita
- 1 1/2 oz tequila
- 1 oz lime juice
- 1/2 oz agave syrup

Shake all ingredients in a tin, pour into a rocks glass. Garnish with a dehydrated lime.
